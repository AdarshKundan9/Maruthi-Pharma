WELCOME

We introduce selves as one of the pioneers in the fight against the menace
of one of mankind’s dreaded ailments Asthma and its co-related ailments.
We manufacture a host of ayurvedic medicines to battle and keep at bay
the impact of asthma & various allergen ailments.



Since we made our entry in the early 1990's, our medicines have been
received well & we feel pleased to inform that they have provided a
considerable amount of relief and help patients lead a near medical
problem-free lives. Our medicines have got better with passage of time
as we maintain a neat communication channel with our clients, get to
know their viewpoints & incorporate their feelings & useful advice in our
manufacturing process.
